Piccut

Piccut is a simple app to make your photos more beautiful and amazing using some of the tools available on it, These tools cut the backgrounds and change them to other backgrounds available within the application, or even change them to transparent, white or any other color you prefer.

Features

Background Eraser

You can select any image from the photo gallery and start removing unwanted areas manually from the image completely and replace those deleted places later.

Background Photo Editor

You can combine your photos and install them up to a maximum of 5 pictures with wallpapers that you download from the market or a specific image from the gallery.

Remove unwanted objects

Using this tool, it will be easy to remove any object, regardless of whether it is a person, writing, poster, watermark or logo. Once you erase it.

Auto Cut

An easy-to-use tool that only determines the foreground and the tool automatically cuts the background of the image professionally and with high precision.
